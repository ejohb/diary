My general condition of unfitness and laziness has made me neglect my prayers lately. I've had a relapse back to my selfishness & temper (not a very serious one, but sufficient to be very troublesome) I must be careful, as well, not to let unselfishness and charity take the form of merely not grabbing things for myself, for instance, when we have to share rations just sitting back and letting the other fellow take the bigger piece. Have been thinking a good deal about hobbies lately. Stamp collecting and then photography seem very attractive to me now.
