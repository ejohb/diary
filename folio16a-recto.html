Wednesday, September 16th Has been unpleasantly windy today and not conducive to being very active. We had a hot meal again today. Though it was only pea soup it tasted very good. I know I feel more hungry again now on the shorter rations. Spend an interesting hour at the "Quiet Time" sharing this morning. These really are helpful and I've learnt quite a bit from hearing what other people have thought about and pray for. Some of the things may seem a little petty but of course all things are important so far as out relations with others are concerned. Still I'd like to see some thoughts on the big questions of life though I'm very vague about this myself. The R.A.F. came over and bombed the town at about 1 o'clock seemingly taking everyone by surprise.
Thursday, September 17th Had a talk with Errol Phipson this morning about religious matters chiefly talked about ways of living and of sin.
